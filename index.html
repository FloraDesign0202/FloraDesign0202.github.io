01 — The Thesis

The average closet holds 100+ pieces.
We wear 20% of them.

Clova turns a phone camera into a personal stylist. Photograph a garment, let AI cut it out and tag it, and it lands in a beautiful digital closet — sorted by category and color, not brand. Then style flat-lay looks from what you already own, find the gaps worth filling, and never forget a piece again.

01

Capture

Snap any item. AI removes the background, identifies the category, color and season, and files it instantly.

02

Organize

A living closet sorted by category and color family — searchable, editable, and honest about what you own.

03

Style

Drag pieces onto a flat-lay board, build looks for any occasion, and save them to a personal lookbook.
